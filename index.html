About

I'm a M.S./B.S. engineering student at the University of Pennsylvania, where I study topics related to Robotics, Electrical Engineering, and Computer Science.

My interests include:

  • Robotic and Cyber-Physical Systems
  • Planning and Control
  • Formal Methods and Verification
  • Embedded Systems
More generally, I like to think about how we can design better physical systems through determining more powerful theoretic abstractions

In a previous life, I spent a lot of time exploring many different aspects of computer science — everything from Lisp machines to computer graphics. I still maintain a casual interest in those topics, and they might make an appearance here as well.
