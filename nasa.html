Mission Planner



In the spring of 2016, I completed Penn's course on advanced robotics. This challenging class required developing an understanding for the theory behind prominent planning, control, and perception algorithms for robotic systems as well as implementing them in a series of projects.

During the course of the semester, I developed a

  • model-based controller capable of stabalizing a quadrotor during aggressive maneuvers.
  • trajectory generator capable of routing a quadrotor about fixed obstacles in 3D space.
  • optical pose and velocity estimator using a monocular camera.
  • simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM) system using an error-state Kalman filter.
